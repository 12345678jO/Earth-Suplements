Our Story

Founded on November 3, 2023, with our store in the city of Hortolândia, in the interior of São Paulo, Earth Supplements is a reseller of supplements from several other famous and quality brands, always seeking to offer greater price accessibility for our customers and security in your purchases.

Missão

Provide easy and uncomplicated access to high-quality supplements, promoting the health and well-being of our customers. We constantly seek to offer a wide variety of products from renowned brands, ensuring excellence and confidence in each purchase.

Valores

Be recognized as the preferred choice of consumers looking for affordable and effective supplements. We intend to expand our reach, making Earth Supplements a reference in the market, maintaining an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction and business integrity.
